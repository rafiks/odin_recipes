Spaghetti

Mama's special spaghetti recipe

My mom has a very special recipe of spaghetti. She always made them when we were kids. I can always remember her making this during Christmas, New Year or just about any special occasions.

It has that distinctive filipino spaghetti taste but with an added richness to it.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodle
  • Ketchup
  • Ground beef

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles
  2. Cook the sauce
  3. Mix the noodle and the sauce
