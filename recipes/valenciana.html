Valenciana

Valenciana ala Bacolod

Valenciana is a staple at a Bacoleno's feast.

I have not seen this dish in any Cebuano speaking regions. I believe this is distinctly a Negrosanon tradition.

Ingredients

  • Atchuete
  • Sticky Rice
  • Liver
  • peas
  • Seafood

Steps

  1. Saute the meat and seafood.
  2. Add the broth. Bring to a boil.
  3. Add the rice. Bring to boil. Mix occasionally.
