Caldereta

Ralph's Caldereta

Nothing special about this recipe. It's just my version of a classic pinoy.

Ingredients

  • beef
  • caldereta mix
  • potato
  • carrots
  • red and green bellpepper

Steps

  1. Saute beef
  2. Add the other ingredients
  3. Add the caldereta mix, water, and boil
  4. Simmer until beef is tender
